Hello!

I'm Anthony Lawn, or "The Paper Pilot", and I develop free, open source programs and games, using a variety of languages and engines. I'm currently working towards my B.S. in Computer Science, after which I hope to go on to getting a Masters degree, with a focus on Data Science.

I really liked playing free online games growing up, but most of those sites have died. Armor games peaked in 2010! There needs to be more of those kind of sites, those kind of games, to inspire the next generation of game developers and computer scientists. That's why I want to make free online games anyone can play. No credit cards or even admin privileges required. All free, and open source. Enjoy :)
